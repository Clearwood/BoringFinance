What is this?

Everytime someone trades on SushiSwap, 0.05% will go to the SushiMaker. The SushiMaker converts this into SUSHI and sends it to the SushiBar.

When you stake SUSHI into the SushiBar, you get xSUSHI. When you leave the bar, you get your SUSHI back plus your share of the extra SUSHI from the SushiMaker.

Currently SushiSwap doesn't yet send the fee to the SushiMaker. This will be enabled sometime after migration.
